Country-Specific Site Design

Web Design

With just a product feed, the Localised platform creates completely transactional, translated & secure mobile-first ecommerce sites for any brand & supported country. We conform to our clients' brand guidelines, but optimize our sites for their respective markets, presenting an entire purchase journey that aligns with what popular local retailers present.

Translation & Content

Using human translators, we translate, adapt and properly contextualize all product descriptions, site experience elements, messaging & offers throughout your site and all customer touch points. The end product is a site that feels thoughtfully translated, not one translated by a machine.

In-Market Customer Acquisition & Conversion Optimization

Ecommerce Store Management

Regardless of your customer's preferred language, we listen to their input about the online experience. We also analyze your shoppers' online data and A/B test to optimize conversion.

Creative Marketing

Our web designers and UX teams localize your online assets in full compliance with your brand guidelines. We adapt and contextualize artwork, merchandising, emails, landing pages and campaigns to resonate with the local consumer, while respecting your brand standards.

Acquisition Marketing

Our digital marketers help you find your target customers, and offer a full range of digital marketing services, including SEO, PPC, affiliate and other country-specific channels.

Localised Merchandising & Pricing

Merchandising

Our merchandising teams help identify top-selling and popular merchandise in order to drive relevancy, engagement, and sales. We create offers, promotions and merchandising strategies that only a merchandising team immersed in the market could effectively create.

Global Pricing

Localised can either adapt and align with an existing foreign currency price book for any brand, or dynamically create rounded, in-currency prices, inclusive of duty & VAT for any market. In either case, prices are always presented at the product detail page level as inclusive of the costs a consumer would expect to see up front. No sticker shock at checkout.

Global Operations & Logistics

Customer Care

Localised customer care provides in-country and in-language customer care via channels and technologies familiar to the consumer (email, chat, social, phone), both pre-sale and post-sale. Your customers can communicate with someone during local business hours.

Global Logistics & Fulfillment

For brands that wish to fulfill global demand from their domestic warehouses, Localised offers a full cross-border export service, orchestrating customs clearance, last mile transportation, and export compliance all from a US facility you domestically ship all parcels to. Alternatively, for brands that already have in-country or in-region distribution, we're happy to let you ship direct. We can make global logistics easy if you want us to, but not if you don't need our help.

Global Payments

Customers check out in their own currency, with guaranteed order totals and prevalent in-market payment options. Localised serves as merchant of record for all transactions, posting and capturing in foreign currencies, protecting the brand against chargebacks and fraud, and insulating the brand from foreign exchange risk by settling with them in their home currency.

Global Compliance

Localised manages export compliance, privacy compliance, and offers the lowest-risk way to establish a meaningful localised operation at scale.
